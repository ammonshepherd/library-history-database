Other files to be added in 1998 include:

Private Libraries [5000+]    Added July 1998

A listing of all private libraries so far identified for the period from ca. 1500 to ca. 1850. Includes references to sale catalogues, inventories, wills, &c.

Country & Town House Libraries [4000+]    Added July 1998

The first attempt at a listing of country and town houses in the British Isles from the late medieval period to ca. 1850. Includes the families associated with each house, and a separate index of family names.

Family Names Index [7000+]    Added July 1998

A companion file to the listing of country and town houses.

Population Statistics [Country - County - Town]

Brings together population statistics for the British Isles from  ca. 1641 to the census of 1851. There will be links with the files for the number of libraries in counties, towns, and villages.

County Maps [ca. 1700 - ca. 1850]

A collection of maps of the British Isles showing roads, railways, canals, topographical features, &c.
